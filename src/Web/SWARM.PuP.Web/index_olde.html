PartyUpPlayer

Solving the LFG Problem

PartyUpPlayer is a free app that helps you find people to game with. Need to get through a dungeon but can't find the right tank? Bored playing alone ‘cause your friends aren’t into the same games? PUP lets you easily find gamers you want to play with.

Beta access coming some time in 2014. Shoot us your email and we'll keep you posted.


Deploy

PUP is easy to use. You simply pick a game and join a game session or schedule one for some time in the future with who you're looking to game with, and let PUP do the rest.

PartyUp

Whether you're an individual or small group you can now find play sessions based on game, interest, and type of player instead of just randoms. You can also save gamers you've like playing with.

Profiles for gaming

Knowing how many hours you've played Skyrim is great, but PUP profiles tell other gamers who and what you're looking for, the games you have and want to play as well your play style and interests.

XBOX | PSN | Steam Integration

Tap to connect all the major networks and we'll be able to recommend when and who you'd probably like to play with (coming soon).
